How to Use
  1. 1
    Upload Your Document
    Drop or select a PDF, DOCX, XLSX, TXT, RTF, ODT, or PPTX file.
  2. 2
    Review Detected Language
    Sabio AI automatically identifies the source language and confidence score.
  3. 3
    Select Target Language
    Choose from 60+ languages organised by region.
  4. 4
    Translate
    Click the translate button and watch the progress bar.
  5. 5
    Download Your Translation
    Pick your preferred format: PDF, DOCX, TXT, RTF, HTML, or ODT.
  6. 6
    Light & Dark Mode
    Toggle the ☀️ / 🌙 button in the navbar to switch themes.
